### Regulatory Environment

#### Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020
- Doctor Registration: RMP registration mandatory
- Consultation Modes: Video, audio, text (with limitations)
- Prescription Rules: Digital signatures required
- First Consultation: In-person preferred for new patients
- Follow-ups: Telemedicine allowed
- Controlled Substances: Cannot be prescribed via telemedicine

#### Personal Data Protection Bill (DPDP Act 2023)
- Data Consent: Explicit consent required
- Data Localization: Health data must be stored in India
- User Rights: Access, correction, deletion
- Breach Notification: Within 72 hours
- Penalties: Up to ₹250 crores for violations

#### NDHM/ABDM Compliance
- Health ID Integration: Mandatory for digital health platforms
- Health Records Interoperability: FHIR standards
- Consent Management: User-controlled consent framework
- Data Security: Encryption, access controls
- Doctor Verification: Integration with National Medical Register
