# SwasthAI Startup Blueprint - Requirements Document

## Introduction

SwasthAI is a comprehensive Indian health-tech startup that merges rural telehealth with diagnostics and an AI-powered prescription scanner with symptom and diet assistance. The platform addresses critical healthcare gaps in both urban and rural India through an integrated, offline-first, multilingual solution that combines OCR-based prescription scanning, AI symptom triage, Indian diet coaching, telemedicine, micro-diagnostics, and ASHA/ANM workflow support.

This requirements document outlines the complete startup blueprint needed to transform SwasthAI from concept to a fundable, executable business plan ready for investor presentation and operational launch.

## Glossary

- **SwasthAI**: The unified health-tech platform combining rural telehealth, diagnostics, and AI health assistance
- **ASHA**: Accredited Social Health Activist - community health workers in rural India
- **ANM**: Auxiliary Nurse Midwife - frontline health workers in primary health centers
- **OCR**: Optical Character Recognition - technology to extract text from images
- **PHC**: Primary Health Center - government-run rural health facilities
- **NDHM**: National Digital Health Mission - India's digital health ecosystem initiative
- **ABDM**: Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission - India's health ID and records program
- **EHR**: Electronic Health Record - digital patient health information system
- **TAM/SAM/SOM**: Total Addressable Market / Serviceable Addressable Market / Serviceable Obtainable Market
- **LTV**: Lifetime Value - total revenue expected from a customer
- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost - cost to acquire one customer
- **GTM**: Go-To-Market - strategy for launching and selling products
- **IVR**: Interactive Voice Response - automated phone system for voice interaction
- **BMI**: Body Mass Index - measure of body fat based on height and weight
- **Blueprint Document**: The comprehensive startup plan covering vision, market, product, business model, and execution strategy
